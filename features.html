Key Features of the System

Empowering safety through smart monitoring.

Gas Level Monitoring

Continuously scans for dangerous gas concentrations and instantly flags threats with visual indicators. Enhances home, lab, and industrial safety.

Temperature Detection

Monitors real-time temperature using high-accuracy sensors. Prevents overheating in sensitive environments with instant alerts.

Humidity Monitoring

Tracks moisture levels in the atmosphere to maintain climate balance in agriculture, greenhouses, and storage facilities.
